About SUR420

Multicultural Music • Community • Culture • Brisbane

Who We Are

SUR420 is a Brisbane-based multicultural music and community organisation dedicated to celebrating diversity, unity, and artistic expression. As an unincorporated Not‑For‑Profit (NFP) community organisation, SUR420 operates entirely through volunteer effort and community support. We do not rely on government grants — all proceeds from our events are reinvested directly into community initiatives, cultural activities, and programs that uplift local talent.

Founded as a grassroots community initiative, SUR420 has grown into a vibrant platform for singers, performers, families, and cultural enthusiasts across Brisbane. Our events showcase talent from diverse backgrounds and help strengthen the multicultural fabric of Queensland.

Our Mission

We aim to promote cultural harmony, social cohesion, and community engagement through music-based events. SUR420 provides opportunities for performers of all ages and backgrounds to share their talent, connect with audiences, and celebrate cultural heritage.

What We Do

  • 🎤 Community karaoke concerts
  • 🎶 Cultural music gatherings
  • 🤝 Collaborative events with local artists
  • 🌏 Multicultural celebrations
  • 🪔 Diwali and festival-themed concerts

Our Community

SUR420 is proud to have built a warm, supportive, and diverse community of performers, families, volunteers, and cultural supporters. Our events consistently draw enthusiastic audiences and create lasting memories.
