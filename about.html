About SUR420

Multicultural Music • Community • Culture • Brisbane

Who We Are

SUR420 is a Brisbane based multicultural music and community organisation dedicated to celebrating diversity, unity, and artistic expression. As an unincorporated Not‑For‑Profit (NFP) community organisation, SUR420 operates entirely through volunteer effort and community support. We do not rely on government grants with all proceeds from our events being reinvested directly into community initiatives, cultural activities, and programs that uplift local talent.

Founded as a grassroots community initiative, SUR420 has grown into a vibrant platform for singers, performers, families, and cultural enthusiasts across Brisbane. Our events showcase talent from diverse backgrounds and help strengthen the multicultural fabric of Queensland.

Our Mission

We aim to promote cultural harmony, social cohesion, and community engagement through music-based events. SUR420 provides opportunities for performers of all ages and backgrounds to share their talent, connect with audiences, and celebrate cultural heritage. As a community-driven NFP, our mission goes beyond entertainment, we are committed to supporting cultural development, empowering local artists, and creating inclusive spaces where everyone feels welcome, with every event contributing directly to community benefit.

Our Community Commitment

SUR420 is deeply committed to giving back to the community. As an unincorporated Not‑For‑Profit organisation that operates without government grants, we rely entirely on community support, volunteer effort, and event proceeds to make a positive impact. Our upcoming initiatives include:

  • 🎁 Visiting the Queensland Children’s Hospital (Lady Cilento) to donate gifts and bring joy to young patients.
  • 📚 Supporting under‑resourced children who lack access to essential school materials and learning resources.
  • 🎓 Launching a scholarship program to help deserving students continue their education when financial barriers stand in the way.
These initiatives reflect our belief that music, culture, and community care go hand in hand — and that together, we can uplift those who need it most.
