Why Sponsor SUR420?

SUR420 hosts multicultural music events that bring together families, performers, and community members from across Brisbane. Sponsoring our events helps promote cultural harmony, artistic expression, and community engagement.

Your support enables us to deliver high-quality cultural programs, empower local performers, and create memorable experiences for audiences.

Sponsorship Benefits

  • 📣 Brand visibility on event banners and promotional materials
  • 🎤 Mentions during events and stage announcements
  • 📱 Social media promotion across SUR420 platforms
  • 🎟 Complimentary tickets for your team
  • 🤝 Community engagement and cultural support recognition
